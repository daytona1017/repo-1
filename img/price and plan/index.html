Frequently Asked Questions

Do I get a free domain name?

We do not offer domain name registration at the moment. Hence cannot offer free domain name as an add-on to your hosting plan.

Can I host multiple domain names with my account?

All our plans have support for multiple domain names and subdomains. Choose the plan that best meet your requirements and start deploying.

I will like to be billed monthly. Will it affect my websites?

No it will not so long you keep paying for the next month after the first one. In the event you’re unable to pay, we will keep your websites running for two weeks (14 days) from the day your subscription expired. If you still haven’t made payment after this period, we will suspend your hosting account but all your files will be intact. Please note that we will not keep your files for more than a month should you decide not to renew your subscription.

I already own a website. Can I transfer it to your web hosting?

Yes you can transfer your website. Our support team are always on ground to help you carry out the transfer; it takes within a few minutes to an hour depending on the size of your website.

I’ve heard about Docker. Can I use it with my hosting account?

For now, only the hosting plan for teams and businesses have access to two (2) and seven (7) docker containers respectively. However they can only use it on demand. We are working towards making it available to all our plans.

I just bought an hosting plan, now what?

We are glad to have you onboard. Point your domain to our server using ns1.leanstackhost.com and ns2.leanstackhost.com as your nameservers. Check our knowledgebase for how to point your domain to our servers from your preferred domain registrar.
